---
title: "Understanding Assistants"
description: "Continue offers two ways to configure your AI assistants"
---

Continue offers flexible AI assistants that can be configured to match your development workflow. You can choose between:

- **Hub Assistants** - Pre-configured assistants that sync across your IDE sessions
- **Local Assistants** - Fully customizable assistants managed through config.yaml

For detailed information about configuring assistants, including setup instructions and choosing between Hub and Local assistants, see our [configuration deep dive](/customize/deep-dives/configuration).
